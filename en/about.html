THE WEBSITE

This website is an interactive exploration of the katana, an iconic Japanese sword. Its goal is to help you discover this art object in all its aspects, offering a smooth and engaging experience to learn more about its history and martial art.

The objective is to spark interest and provide an informative resource about the katana, while reflecting on its legacy and future.

WHO AM I?

Hi! My name is Mathis Juillard, and I am a second-year student in the Multimedia and Internet Professions program (MMI). This site is a project exploring the philosophy and history of the katana.

Through this website, I want to introduce you to the history, meaning, and cultural importance of the katana. You can explore links with various themes, from Japanese culture to your own life and history, without seeing the katana from a single perspective. Welcome to my world!

WHY?

This website was created to explore the history and symbolism of the katana through cinema, music, and the arts. An adventure through various themes.

It aims to showcase this iconic object from a new perspective, exploring its meaning and resonance in the modern world.

The goal is to spark visitors' interest in the katana and provide an interactive experience that stimulates reflection on the legacy and meaning of this legendary object.
